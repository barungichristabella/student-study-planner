Study Tips

Simple tips to help you study smarter and perform better.

Top Study Tips for Students

1. Plan Your Study Time

Set a specific time each day for studying. Consistency is key. Even 30 minutes a day adds up over time.

2. Break It Into Small Chunks

Don't try to study everything at once. Break your work into smaller parts and take short breaks in between.

3. Remove Distractions

Put your phone away and find a quiet place to study. Even 20 minutes of focused study is better than 2 hours of distracted reading.

4. Review Your Notes Often

Don't wait until exam time. Go over your notes regularly so the information stays fresh in your mind.

5. Get Enough Sleep

Your brain needs rest to remember what you studied. Staying up all night before an exam usually does more harm than good.
